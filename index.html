about me


Hi, there! My name is Mojin Yu.

My favorite childhood activity was making games in Microsoft Powerpoint. To make sure my little sister could play my games, I spent a lot of time adjusting layouts and interactions so that they would make sense to a preschooler. This experience sparked my interest in understanding people's different perceptions of the world and creating things that recognize the needs of specific audiences.

Currently, I am a senior at the University of Rochester, majoring in Digital Media Studies and Psychology. I'm passionate about design, and I hope to use the empathy I've developed through my background in social science to solve challenging problems.
