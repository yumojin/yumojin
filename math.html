About my work

Since April 2020, I've been working as the UX designer on the Microsoft Math Solver team. Microsoft Math Solver is an AI-powered education product that helps students learn to solve math problems. Right now, there are over 2 million active users of Math Solver worldwide.

As the only designer on the product, I...

  • Generate low to high fidelity prototypes, mockups, illustrations, and motion graphics
  • Solve complex interaction problems across various platforms, including mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • Lead design iterations based on user feedback
  • Collaborate with engineers and PMs daily to deliver polished experiences

If you are interested in learning more about what and how I design at Math Solver, please reach out to me via email !
